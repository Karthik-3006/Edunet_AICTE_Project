Interview Preparation Guidelines

Follow these steps to get the most out of your AI-powered prep

📄

Upload Your Resume

Start by uploading your resume or CV in any format — PDF, Word (.docx), image (PNG/JPG), or plain text. The AI will extract your skills and identify gaps for your target role.

🎯

Select Your Role

Choose a specific job title and experience level. The more precise you are, the more tailored your question set will be.

🏢

Pick a Target Company

Optionally select a target company (IBM, Google, Amazon, etc.) for company-specific question patterns and preparation strategies.

📝

Answer Thoughtfully

Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioural questions. For technical ones, explain your reasoning step by step.

📊

Review Your Feedback

After each answer, review the IBM Granite evaluation — score, strengths, weaknesses, and the ideal model answer.

🔁

Iterate & Improve

Repeat sessions with different question types. Use the progress dashboard to track weak areas and focus your revision accordingly.

Tips for Best Results

  • Practice in a quiet environment to simulate real interview conditions.
  • Time yourself — aim for 2–3 minutes per answer for most questions.
  • Don't skip the feedback card; it contains your most actionable improvement points.
  • Try all three interview types: HR, Technical, and Mixed, across multiple sessions.
  • For technical roles, also practice on coding platforms (LeetCode, HackerRank) alongside mock interviews.
  • Review the suggested ideal answers and rewrite them in your own words for retention.

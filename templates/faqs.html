Everything you need to know about the Interview Trainer Agent

The Interview Trainer Agent is an AI-powered mock interview platform built on IBM Granite and Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG). It generates tailored interview questions based on your profile and provides instant, detailed feedback on your answers.
The platform is powered by IBM Granite (IBM Cloud Lite services) and uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to retrieve up-to-date, role-specific information from interview guides, professional databases, and curated knowledge sources before generating responses.
Three interview types are supported:
  • HR Round — Behavioural and soft-skills questions.
  • Technical — Role-specific in-depth technical questions.
  • Mixed — A combination of HR and Technical questions.
Each answer is sent to IBM Granite which evaluates it and returns:
  • A score out of 10
  • List of strengths in your answer
  • Areas to improve
  • Overall feedback summary
  • A model ideal answer for comparison
Yes! The platform supports company-specific preparation. When you select a target company, the AI retrieves company-relevant interview topics, common question patterns, and tailored preparation suggestions based on that company's known interview style.
The "Start Now" section is designed for freshers and entry-level candidates. It provides a complete AI-generated preparation roadmap with daily and weekly goals, recommended books, study notes, coding resources, and a progress dashboard — guiding you from preparation to placement.
Your session data is stored temporarily in your browser session only for the duration of the interview. It is not persisted to any external database or shared with third parties. Each new interview starts a fresh session.
The platform includes a graceful fallback mechanism. If the IBM Granite API is unavailable or credentials are not configured, the system falls back to a curated static question bank and stub evaluation responses so you can still practice without interruption.
